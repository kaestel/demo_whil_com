1. Ready

Replace that clutter with a serious intent to achieve something real – big, small, or medium – that lies in the direction of your greatness.

Then take a final deep breath, open your eyes, and get going before that bus hits you.

2. Aim

Find a simple black dot in your mind. Any size. Watch it for 5 to 45 seconds to clear your brain of cravings, commodity prices and childhood fears.

3. Go!

Close your eyes, and breathe in deeply three times. Two if you’re in a huge hurry.

Whil to meditate

A core tool of Whil is a 60-second meditation technique to renew your will and refocus your where. A mental reboot. Unlike once-a-week therapy, or long-form meditation Whil is instantly accessible.

No yoga mat required. Batteries included.

Whil meditation is so quick and easy you can do it: while you pee, while you wait for the #11 bus, while the Sumatra drips, while you are putting on your shoes.

Once a day is a start. Three times a day is definitely going to help. Five times a day is a healthy obsession.
